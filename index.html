How It Works

The platform
for people who compete.

VORSO wraps fitness in structured challenge — games with stakes, sessions with meaning, and a platform built to push you further every time.

01

Draw a Card

Each card maps to an exercise and rep count. Ace of spades? 14 push-ups. Jack of hearts? 11 squats.

02

Do the Reps

Hit start, complete the exercise, tap done. The timer tracks every card and every second — no fudging.

03

04

Compete & Connect

Climb global and friends-only leaderboards, follow other athletes, react to their sessions, and challenge anyone to beat your score.

FAQ

Questions answered.

Everything you want to know before it drops.

Do I need any equipment?+
None. Every exercise in VORSO uses your bodyweight — push-ups, squats, lunges, sit-ups and more. You just need a bit of floor space and something to prove.
Is it free?+
Yes. VORSO is free to download with no subscription. Both games — Card Battle Arena and The Call — are fully unlocked from day one.
How does multiplayer work?+
Card Battle Arena supports 2–4 players on a single device. Everyone does the same card each round — one person goes at a time while the rest rest. The Call is a 1v1 game where both players compete in the same session, sealing their rep commitments before each round.
Can I compete with friends?+
Yes — VORSO is social. Every session ranks you on global and friends-only leaderboards by reps and time. Follow other athletes, see their sessions and posts in your feed, cheer each other on with kudos and comments, and challenge anyone to beat your best. Keep your profile public, or go private and approve who follows you. Your real name is never shown — only the username you choose.
What's The Call?+
The Call is a precision rep game. Before you start, you predict exactly how many reps you'll do. Then you do them. Hit your number exactly and you score max points. Go over — that's a bust, and it's punished hard. Round 5 is Bold Mode: double your stake or fold. It rewards people who actually know their body.
What platforms will VORSO be on?+
iOS first, launching July 19. Android and a web version are on the roadmap — join the waitlist and we'll keep you posted.
Can I create my own exercises?+
Yes. Card Battle Arena lets you create and assign custom exercises to any suit — so if your thing is pull-ups or box jumps, you're covered.
