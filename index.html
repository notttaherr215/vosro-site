How It Works

The platform
for people who compete.

VORSO wraps fitness in structured challenge — games with stakes, sessions with meaning, and a platform built to push you further every time.

01

Draw a Card

Each card maps to an exercise and rep count. Ace of spades? 14 push-ups. Jack of hearts? 11 squats.

02

Do the Reps

Hit start, complete the exercise, tap done. The timer tracks every card and every second — no fudging.

03

Beat Your Best

Personal bests, daily streaks, XP, levelling — from Rookie to Legend. Every session counts.

04

Challenge Others

Multiplayer on one device or head-to-head in The Call — a precision rep prediction game.

Two Games. One App.

Pick your mode.

Solo grind or competitive edge — VORSO has a game for every kind of athlete.

Game 1

Card Battle Arena

Work through a shuffled deck of 13, 26, or 52 cards. Solo practice or local multiplayer — fastest total time wins.

  • Solo & multiplayer (2–4 players)
  • 42 bodyweight exercises
  • Personal bests & streak tracking
  • XP levelling from Rookie to Legend
  • Session goals & custom exercises
Game 2

The Call

Call your reps before you do them. Precision wins — and the asymmetric scoring punishes sandbaggers.

  • 1v1 precision rep prediction
  • 5 rounds with escalating multipliers
  • Sealed commit — no peeking
  • Bold mode: double or bust on Round 5
  • Full scorecard & rematch
