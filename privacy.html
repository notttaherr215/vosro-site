Privacy Policy

Last updated June 12, 2026

What We Collect

VORSO collects your email address when you create an account. This is used solely to send you a one-time sign-in code (OTP). We do not collect your name, location, health data, or any other personal information.

Your Workout Data

All workout history, personal bests, streaks, and exercise data are stored on your device only. This data is never transmitted to our servers or shared with any third party.

Authentication

Sign-in is handled via AWS Cognito, a secure authentication service provided by Amazon Web Services. Your email address is stored by AWS Cognito solely for the purpose of sending sign-in codes.

Third Parties

We do not sell, share, or rent your personal information to any third party. We do not use advertising networks or analytics services that collect personal data.

Data Deletion

You can delete all locally stored data at any time via Settings → Clear All Data within the app. To request deletion of your account and email address from our systems, contact us at the address below.

Children

VORSO is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.
