Privacy Policy

Last updated: 23 June 2026

VORSO is a fitness platform built around card-based games. We believe your health data belongs to you. This policy explains exactly what we collect, why, and what rights you have over it.

1. Who We Are

VORSO ("we", "us", "our") operates the VORSO mobile application and the website at vorso.org. For any privacy enquiries, contact us at customerfeedback@vorso.org.

2. What We Collect and Why

Data Purpose Where stored Retention
Email address Send sign-in OTP codes; identify your account AWS Cognito (EU / US-East) Until you delete your account
Display name (chosen by you) Personalise your in-app experience AWS Cognito Until you delete your account
Workout history, scores, streaks Show your personal bests and progress Your device only (local storage) Until you clear data in-app
Feedback messages Improve the product; respond to you Our email inbox (customerfeedback@vorso.org) Deleted periodically; no longer than 2 years
Waitlist submissions (website) Notify you when new features launch Our email inbox Until you ask to be removed

We do not collect location data, health metrics, device identifiers, or any biometric data. We do not use third-party analytics SDKs (no Firebase, Mixpanel, or equivalent).

3. Your Workout Data Stays on Your Device

All game scores, personal bests, workout history, and streaks are stored exclusively on your device using local app storage. This data is never sent to our servers and is not accessible to us. If you delete the app, this data is permanently removed.

You can clear all locally stored data at any time via Settings → Data within the app.

4. Third-Party Services We Use

We use a small number of carefully selected services:

  • AWS Cognito (Amazon Web Services) — handles account creation and sign-in authentication. Your email address is stored in Cognito. Amazon's privacy policy: aws.amazon.com/privacy
  • Resend — delivers OTP sign-in codes and feedback confirmation emails. Email content passes through Resend's infrastructure transiently and is not retained by them. Resend's privacy policy: resend.com/legal/privacy-policy
  • Cloudflare — proxies our website for security and performance. Cloudflare may process request metadata (IP address, headers) per their privacy policy: cloudflare.com/privacypolicy

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with any other third party for marketing or commercial purposes.

5. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

If you are in the European Economic Area (EEA), UK, or Switzerland, we process your personal data under the following legal bases:

  • Contract performance — your email address is necessary to provide the authentication service you signed up for.
  • Legitimate interests — responding to feedback you voluntarily submit.
  • Consent — waitlist sign-ups; you can withdraw at any time by contacting us.

6. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights:

  • Access — request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correction — ask us to correct inaccurate data.
  • Deletion ("right to be forgotten") — ask us to delete your account and all associated personal data.
  • Portability — receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format.
  • Objection / Restriction — object to or restrict certain processing activities.
  • California (CCPA) — California residents have the right to know, delete, and opt-out of the sale of personal information. We do not sell personal information.

To exercise any of these rights, email customerfeedback@vorso.org. We will respond within 30 days.

7. Data Security

Authentication is managed through AWS Cognito, which enforces industry-standard security including encrypted storage and TLS in transit. Our website is served over HTTPS with HSTS enforced. We do not store passwords — sign-in uses one-time codes only.

8. Children

VORSO is designed for users aged 13 and over. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13 (or under 16 in the EEA). If you believe a child has provided us with their data, contact us and we will delete it promptly.

9. Changes to This Policy

If we make material changes to this policy, we will update the "Last updated" date above and, where appropriate, notify you by email. Continued use of VORSO after changes take effect constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
