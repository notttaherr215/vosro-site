Support & Help

Need a hand with VORSO? We're a small team and we read every message.

Contact us

Email customerfeedback@vorso.org — we aim to reply within 2 business days. For reports of abuse or objectionable content, we act within 24 hours.

Common questions

How do I delete my account?

In the app, open the Profile tab and tap Delete my account at the bottom. This permanently deletes your account, username, scores, posts, comments, and follows from our servers. You can also email us to request deletion.

How do I report or block someone?

Tap the ••• menu on any profile or post (or press and hold a comment) to Report or Block. Blocking stops that person from following you, seeing you in friend leaderboards, or interacting with you. We review all reports and act within 24 hours.

How do I stop appearing on leaderboards?

Open Settings and turn off “Appear on leaderboards.” You'll be removed from all leaderboards immediately. You can also make your whole account Private so people must request to follow you.

How do I change my username, bio, or details?

Profile tab → Edit Profile. Usernames must be unique (3–20 letters, numbers, or underscores).

I'm having trouble signing in

VORSO uses a passwordless sign-in: we email you a one-time code. Check your spam folder, make sure the email address is correct, and request a new code if it expires. Still stuck? Email us.

Is my data private?

Your real name and email are never shown to other users — only your chosen username. Your workout history stays on your device and your private iCloud. See our Privacy Policy for the full details.

How old do I have to be?

VORSO is for adults — you must be 18 or older to use it. See our Terms of Service.

Safety

VORSO has zero tolerance for objectionable content or abusive behaviour. If you see something that breaks our rules, report it in the app or email customerfeedback@vorso.org. Our community rules are in the Terms of Service.
